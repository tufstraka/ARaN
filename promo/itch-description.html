📖 Description (for itch.io)

You are a robot. The factory is going haywire. ESCAPE!

FLIP BOT is a one-button endless runner where you play as a sentient robot escaping a malfunctioning factory. The only control? TAP or SPACE to flip gravity.

Survive crushing pistons, deadly lasers, and electrified spikes as the factory spirals into chaos. Collect gears to unlock permanent upgrades. Chase high scores. Beat your friends.

Features

  • 🎮 One-button gameplay - Simple to learn, hard to master
  • 🔄 Gravity-flipping mechanic - Walk on floors AND ceilings
  • ⚙️ Gear collection - Build combos for massive score multipliers
  • ⬆️ 5 Upgrades - Shields, speed boost, magnet, and more
  • 🏆 8 Difficulty phases - From "Boot Sequence" to "Chaos Mode"
  • 💾 Auto-save - Your progress is saved locally
  • 🎵 Procedural audio - All sounds generated in real-time

How to Play

Your robot auto-runs to the right. Tap or press SPACE to flip gravity. Avoid obstacles. Collect gears. Survive as long as you can!

🎮 Controls

SPACE or TAP = Flip Gravity

That's it. One button.

✨ Features

🔄 Gravity Flip

One tap inverts gravity. Walk on the ceiling. Dodge obstacles by flipping at the right moment.

⚙️ Combo System

Chain gear pickups without missing to build your combo multiplier up to 10x!

⬆️ Upgrades

Spend collected gears on permanent upgrades: shields, speed boosts, magnets, and more.
