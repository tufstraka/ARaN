## Full Description (copy to itch.io)

### 🤖 FLIP BOT - Factory Escape

**You are a robot. The factory is going haywire. ESCAPE!**

FLIP BOT is a one-button endless runner where you play as a sentient robot escaping a malfunctioning factory. The only control? **TAP or SPACE to flip gravity.**

Survive crushing pistons, deadly lasers, and electrified spikes as the factory spirals into chaos. Collect gears to unlock permanent upgrades. Chase high scores. Beat your friends.

---

### ✨ Features

- 🎮 **One-button gameplay** - Simple to learn, hard to master
- 🔄 **Gravity-flipping mechanic** - Walk on floors AND ceilings
- ⚙️ **Gear collection** - Build combos for massive score multipliers (up to 10x!)
- ⬆️ **5 Upgrades** - Shields, speed boost, magnet, score multiplier, combo buffer
- 🏆 **8 Difficulty phases** - From "Boot Sequence" to "Chaos Mode"
- 💾 **Auto-save** - Your progress is saved locally
- 🎵 **Procedural audio** - All sounds generated in real-time (no loading!)

---

### 🎮 How to Play

1. Your robot **auto-runs to the right**
2. **TAP** or press **SPACE** to flip gravity
3. **Avoid obstacles** - spikes, crushers, lasers, gaps
4. **Collect gears** - chain pickups for combo multiplier
5. **Survive as long as you can!**
